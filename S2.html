BIP - Interplay of Landscape, Tectonics and Climate Change

This report summarizes the Blended Intensive Programme (BIP) Interplay of Landscape, Tectonics, and Climate Change in the Mediterranean, hosted by NKUA from 30 June to 4 July 2025. The course examined climate change impacts on tectonic and coastal environments, including coastal erosion, changing precipitation, and rising hazards, combining virtual learning and fieldwork for practical insights into landscape evolution and adaptation.
