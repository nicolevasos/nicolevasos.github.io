This study assessed the effectiveness of a map depicting Arctic sea ice loss from 1979 to 2021. Using a color gradient from light tones to deep reds, it highlighted areas of maximum impact. The research evaluated whether the map’s message was clear and understood by its audience.

Automated detection of soil mass movement using Sentinel-2 imagery through EO-Browser. The utilized script analyzes NDWI, NDVI, and B11 values to identify landslides and movement vectors, aiding disaster management in Kaprun, Salzburg, after severe August 2024 rainfall.
